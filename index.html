Super Mario Odyssey

My thoughts after playing for the first time

I try not to play too many video games nowadays, but I purchased my friend's old Nintendo Switch and have been playing more than I'd like to...

The game I have been playing most recently has been Super Mario Odyssey, so I figured I would include some thoughts on the game.

Things I like
  • The ability to explore
  • The variety of playable characters
  • I changed the hat to a visor
Things I don't like
  • It's pretty easy
  • The levels can get repetitive
